My Favorite Games Ranking

  1. Minecraft

    2. Synopsis

    Minecraft is a game where you build things with blocks in an open and free-to-explore virtual world. It's what gamers call a 'sandbox,' because it works like a sandbox where the only limit for the player is their own imagination and creativity.

  2. Assassin's Creed Saga

    3. Synopsis

    The central premise of the story revolves around the rivalry between two ancient secret societies: the Assassins, who seek peace through free will, and the Templars, who aim to dominate the world and impose order on humanity.

  3. Grand Theft Auto V

    4. Synopsis

    The game is set in the fictional state of San Andreas, with the single-player campaign following three criminals and their efforts to commit heists under the pressure of a government agency. The open world allows players to freely navigate the rural and urban areas of San Andreas.

  4. Skate 3

    5. Synopsis

    The game takes place in the fictional city of Port Carverton, which embraces skateboarding, unlike the "skaters are not criminals" mentality present in the second game. Although the city is more colorful and friendly than Skate 2's less saturated and gray streets, the gameplay remains similar to the first two games.

  5. Hades

    6. Synopsis

    A teenage son (Zagreus, god of blood and prince of the Underworld) tries to escape from home to free himself from an emotionally cold and distant father (Hades, god of the dead and ruler of... well, Hades).
