My Favorite Anime Ranking

  1. Jujutsu Kaisen

    2. Synopsis

    Jujutsu Kaisen is an epic journey that begins with Yuji Itadori, an ordinary high school student whose life is transformed after a fateful encounter with the supernatural. When Yuji accidentally unleashes a terrible curse contained in a cursed artifact, he finds himself immersed in a world of darkness and danger.

  2. Bleach

    3. Synopsis

    In Bleach, Ichigo Kurosaki is an unusual high school student: since childhood, he has been able to see ghosts. His life changes completely when he and his two sisters are attacked by a malevolent spirit and receive help from a shinigami named Rukia Kuchiki.

  3. Black Clover

    4. Synopsis

    A fearless boy without magical powers fights for the title of Wizard King, facing four rivals who were banished and now threaten to destroy the Clover Kingdom.

  4. Demon Slayer

    5. Synopsis

    In Kimetsu no Yaiba, Tanjiro, a kind young man who makes a living selling charcoal, discovers that his family has been massacred by a demon. To make matters worse, Nezuko, his younger sister and the only survivor, has also been turned into a demon.

  5. Chainsaw Man

    6. Synopsis

    Denji is a teenager who lives with Pochita, the Chainsaw Devil. Due to the debts he inherited from his father, he lives in poverty, exterminating other demons with Pochita to pay the bills. Until one day, Denji is betrayed and killed.
